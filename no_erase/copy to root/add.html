Add your family

Do you want to add your family to the tree? If so, I hope these two forms make it easy. You can add two types of entries: a new person; and a new ‘marriage’ (a new marriage also means a new partner of either sex).

Everytime you hit the 'submit' button on a form an email of the details is sent to me at nick@willder.net. Fill in the forms as many times as you wish with as many details as you can, and hit ‘submit’ each time. The submissions are processed by hand, and will not appear in these pages immediately.

To make sure that the families are properly related it is important that you invent a unique person code for each person and a unique marriage code for each ‘marriage’ and stick to it! You may find it easier to print out several copies of the forms fill them in by hand to organise yourself.
