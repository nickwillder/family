Dava writes:
My father served with his two brothers, Thomas and James in WWII. I remember stories that were told of him wanting to be married and have a child so he would have something to fight for during the war. My mother was expecting my eldest sister [Jeanette] when my father went overseas, and it was there that he got the news of her birth via telegram.
