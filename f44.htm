James

Born 1793, Burnaston
Christened 1793, Derby St. Osmund
Died 1874, [Newport, Shropshire]
Occupation: Farmer of Plardiwick
RW#: 1/197
Notes amended 15 Feb 2008
c1815-18: Presumed moved from Colton to Whittington, Lichfield, Staffs.
then possibly to Bowers, Eccleshall, Staffs (possibly wife's parent's house; dtr Eliza and son John born there). Baptised as an adult in Belper, Derbyshire 1816.
c1820-25: Farmer of Plardiwick Hall, Staffs.
c1821-22: Left Church of England. Became Independent Protestant (IDP).
c1825: Moved to Longton, Stoke-on-Trent, Staffs. for short period (possibly staying with sister Lydia).
c1826: Moved to Bilston, Staffs (son Theodopholus born there 1826)
c1841: Schoolmaster of Gnosall Heath, Staffs.
c1848-60: Dairyman of Aston, Birmingham (there in 1851 census. May have resided at Swynnerton, Staffs for a time in this period, and witnessed marriage of Ann Mosedale - Jane's sister?).
c1860-70: Retired to West Derby, Liverpool (there in 1861 census. Son John already living nearby).
c1870-72: Staying at West Kirby, Cheshire (visitor at son-in-law Joseph Janion's Public House).
c1872-73: Retuned to Plardiwick, Staffs.
1874: Died Plardiwick, Staffs.
Note: [Newport Salop] includes a church of Staffs. incl. Gnosall, etc.
